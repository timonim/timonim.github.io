Fanbase

Fostering a community
on Spotify

Music connects people and inspires conversations like no other topic. From album reviews to viral music videos, music sparks passion and arguments among fans around the world. Spotify is the go-to destination for music lovers, but currently lacks a place to share your thoughts. Fanbase, a Twitter-like social media feed within Spotify, gives fans a place to share the music they love - and why they love it - with the Spotify community.

The Problem

Music has become an integral part of our daily lives, with streaming platforms like Spotify leading the way in how we consume and discover new music. However, despite its massive user base and vast music library, Spotify lacks a dedicated social feature that allows users to share and discuss their favorite songs and artists. This absence of a social feature hinders the platform's ability to foster a sense of community among its users, leading to a lack of engagement, loyalty, and ultimately, user retention.

The Billboard Hot Trending Songs chart bases its data on Twitter, which highlights the power of music to bring people together and spark conversations. Music has long been a popular topic on the platform, with users sharing their favorite songs, artists, and playlists with one another.

How can Spotify
increase user engagement?

The Solution

Fanbase is a Twitter-like social media platform within Spotify, allowing users to share their favorite songs with a short text, image, or video. By introducing Fanbase, Spotify could fill a vacuum in the short form social media landscape, providing a unique and innovative way for music enthusiasts to share and discover music. With its large user base, Spotify is well-positioned to capitalize on this opportunity and win over new users who are looking for a fresh and exciting way to engage with music.

Users can search for and post their favorite (or least favorite) songs along with all their thoughts, within 240 characters. Other users can then view, share, and comment on these posts, creating a community of music lovers.
By viewing all the posts that a song has on Fanbase, users can see how it resonates with other listeners, and maybe even form a deeper understanding of a song's broader reception within music trends. They can also use this feature to discover new music and artists that they may not have encountered otherwise.
The feature seamlessly integrates into the Spotify mobile app, allowing users to access Fanbase from anywhere, at any time. This adds a new level of convenience and flexibility to the music streaming experience, making it easier for users to discover new music, connect with fellow fans, and share their love for music on the go.
On Fanbase, users can look at other profiles to see what music they like and what they've been listening to. They can also follow other users to keep up with their musical tastes and interests.
