Locu

Grasshopper protein supplements

Locu was my bachelor's thesis project done in collaboration with Hargol, an Israeli food tech company. The thesis, "Branding Insects: Transitioning Pest to Food" involved research, market analysis, brief writing, branding, 3D modeling, simulation and rendering.

The gummy series depicts an idyllic nature scene that features complementary colors to the packaging. The product aims to appeal towards casual customers looking to enhance their nutrition with a tasty, daily snack.
The direction of the protein bar packaging features the product floating in a mountainous, surreal scene with rocks and boulders flying in the background. The product is aimed towards intermediate trainers and people who lead an active lifestyle.
The project was done in order to conceptualize a grasshopper product while solving its problematic image. As various insect products in this field lean and emphasize the insect element of their product, thus scaring potential customers away, I decided to incorporate it as an ingredient like any other.
