Pharmocann

Medical Cannabis

As a designer at David and Yosef, I focused on creating and designing cannabis products for Israel's leading medical cannabis company, Pharmocann. My specialization included developing and re-imagining a variety of products such as oils, pre-rolls, inflorescences, and even the product catalog itself.

Adapting packaging designs to comply with printing restrictions and regulatory demands mandated by the governing body can be a complex process, especially in the highly regulated cannabis industry. The challenge involves ensuring that the design meets all necessary requirements, while still maintaining a visually appealing and effective product.

Packaging Re-branding

As the lead designer for Pharmocann's rebranding project, I was responsible for revitalizing their existing product line to better appeal to a growing recreational audience. To remain competitive in a highly contested market, Pharmocann recognized the need to revamp their branding strategy, and I led the effort to achieve this goal.
The project involved careful consideration of market trends, consumer preferences, and brand identity. The rebranding required analysis of competitors and new opportunities in the market to target Pharmocann's rebranding efforts.

Product Catalog

Producing an informative brochure was an important part of reaching out to prospective cannabis patients who were unfamiliar with the treatment option. Many of the intended readers would be in difficult circumstances or turning to cannabis out of necessity, rather than choice.

The brochure I created required careful image editing, styled text, layout and print design suited to this audience. The aim was to welcome people who were new or inexperienced with cannabis as a treatment, helping them understand their options in an accessible way. The design needed to convey approachability and compassion for readers who may be struggling.
