I'm Tim Portnoy, a multidisciplinary designer based in Tel Aviv, Israel. I specialize in designing visually stunning and highly functional solutions, particularly for complex products and design systems.

My skills include UI/UX, brand design, typography, AI image generation, marketing, graphic design, and 3D, and I use tools like Figma, Stable Diffusion, Blender, Illustrator, Indesign, Photoshop, Procreate, and more.

I love creating designs that solve problems and impress. Whether it's an app, a brand, or something else, I tailor my designs to be functional and beautiful for every project.

Asides design, I love learning languages, experimental music and perfecting my aeropress technique.

Making this portfolio a reality

Coding My Portfolio from Scratch

As a designer, I realized the importance of understanding how my designs are built. Starting with zero coding knowledge, I took on the challenge to learn frontend development, in order to code my portfolio from the ground up.

Now, my portfolio serves as a testament to not only my design abilities but also my commitment to understanding and implementing the technical foundations that make those designs a reality.

This self-taught coding journey has been crucial in allowing me to deliver complete design solutions that are as functional as they are visually compelling.
