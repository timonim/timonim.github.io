I'm Tim Portnoy, a multidisciplinary designer based in Tel Aviv, Israel. I specialize in designing visually stunning and highly functional solutions, particularly for complex products and design systems.

My skills include UI/UX, brand design, typography, AI image generation, marketing, graphic design, and 3D, and I use tools like Figma, Stable Diffusion, Blender, Illustrator, Indesign, Photoshop, Procreate, and more.

I love creating designs that solve problems and impress. Whether it's an app, a brand, or something else, I tailor my designs to be functional and beautiful for every project.

Asides design, I love languages, jazz and history podcasts.
